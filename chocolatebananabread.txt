This is the best receipe for making delicious and moist chocolate chip banana bread. 

There are 3 key ingredients (one may surprise you). 

1. Extra ripe bananas (the browner the better)

2. milk chocolate cholate chips (chocolate chop and chocolate chunk mix preferred)

3. greek yogurt (this is the suprise ingredient)

The rest of the ingredients are pretty basic. Flour, sugar (granulated and brown), eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda, salt, butter (if using salted butter, don't add salt).
